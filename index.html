Our Facilities

LIBRARY

Nava Nalanda Central Library is housed in a centrally air conditioned spacious premises covering an area of 25,000 square feet. It has over one lakh books (reference books, course materials, text books etc.). The collection includes books on various subjects related to Engineering, Management, Humanities, Social Sciences and Fiction. Other than regular books, the library collection also includes, theses, standards, and bound volumes of journals.

TRACK

Physical recreation through games and sports has been an important part of the Institute since its inception. TIET’s sports section organizes the tournaments in all games like Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, Tennis, Table Tennis, Chess, Handball, Swimming, and Athletics for boys and girls throughout the year.

Tournaments are URJA , Inter Technology University competition , Thaparlympics, SPADES, IGNITE, Annual Athletic Meet, besides this competitions TIET’s students also participate in All India inter university tournaments as well in invitational tournaments organized by the different AIU and IITS.

ATM

Our university provides services of ATMS of SBI, ICICI & HDFC Banks. Students who need cash while commuting can look for outdoor freestanding ATM Machines on College campuses which provide 24 hour a day convenience.
